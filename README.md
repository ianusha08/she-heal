# 🌸 SheHeal

### Your Companion for Hormonal, Menstrual, and Reproductive Health

SheHeal is a women's health companion designed to help users track menstrual cycles, hormonal symptoms, wellness habits, and overall reproductive health in one place.

The goal is to provide a supportive, data-driven platform for managing conditions such as PCOS, PCOD, irregular periods, PMS, hormonal imbalances, and related wellness concerns.

---

## 💡 Vision

Women's health data is often scattered across multiple apps and notes.

SheHeal aims to bring cycle tracking, symptom monitoring, wellness habits, and health insights into a single intuitive platform that empowers users to better understand their bodies.
